Biography of Dasha

Birthday: 21 October 1998

Daria Orlova was born in Kyiv and lived with her mother, stepfather and a younger sister. Her mother Nataliia Orlova had a masters degree in microbiology and became a flight attendant after marrying Daria's stepfather Igor Kostiuchenko, a pilot in FlyUIA. At the age of 6 Daria went to school and became an older sister for Kseniia Kostiuchenko

Apart from school, she did professional ice-skating and volunteered in 2014 in gathering humanitarian aid for the Ukrainian army.

Her first and unpleasant experience in Bogomolets national Medical university ended after 2 years of failed B.S. Daria left the university by her own will, passed a German exam and started her education of science from scratch in Frankfurt am Main.

The Life Now

Boston, Massachusetts

Daria currently studies at BU and works at Harvard Medical School as a member of Smirnakis Lab.

She takes active part in her project Mriya, a non-profit organisation that sends various humanitarian aid for the needs of Ukrainians: soldiers, civilians, children and animals.

Apart from being the parents to a cat and a dog, Daria and Dmytro are waiting for a girl.
